Getting Started Guide

Follow these instructions FOR GREAT JUSTICE!

  • Set Up Your Account
    1. Sign up if you haven't already.
    2. Fill out your profile
    3. Important! To use Twitter, enter your twitter web page url in the "Website" field with nothing trailing, like so:
      http://twitter.com/yourhandle
    4. Add the books you are comfortable lending out. (That signed, first edition, leather-bound Proust may not be appropriate. That paperback book on Rails might be a better bet. And more practically useful. And less imaginary.)

  • Get Your Read On!
    1. Find a book you want to borrow.
    2. Contact the book's owner via the Twitter or email links on the book listing.
    3. If the owner is willing to lend to you (they don't have to) then arrange a mutually agreeable time to grab the book. PLEASE USE COMMON SENSE ABOUT MEETING PEOPLE FROM THE INTERWEBS.
    4. Be generous and loan to others!
